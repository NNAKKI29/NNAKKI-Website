Sandton FAQs

Do you supply invoices for company bookings?

Yes. Choose “Corporate” at checkout and add your billing details. We’ll issue VAT-ready invoices.

How quickly can I book for an event?

Use “Earliest Availability” to find same-day or next-day slots. Concierge can dispatch multiple pros for larger teams.

Is there a travel fee for hotel calls?

Some pros add a travel/parking fee for on-site service. You’ll see this clearly before confirming.

Can I add standby touch-ups?

Yes—add “On-Set Touch-Ups” during checkout to keep your artist/stylist on standby for photos & stage cues.
